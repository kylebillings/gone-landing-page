With the way Slice manages linked mailboxes, your mailbox can only be managed through Slice or through Gone!, but not both.

If you want to unlink your mailbox from Slice you'll have to follow these steps:

  1. Log into www.slice.com
  2. Choose "Account Settings" from the drop-down on the top right.
  3. Choose the "Linked Inboxes" tab.
  4. Choose to delete or unlink your inbox.
  5. Go back to Gone! app and try connecting your email again.
